pub struct NFA {
    pub initial_state: StateId,
    pub final_states: FxHashMap<StateId, BranchId>,
    pub transitions: MultiMap<(StateId, Transition), StateId>,
}

Non-deterministic Finite Automaton.

The inside implementation of the automaton is based on u8, therefore a character transition may be represented as multiple edges in the NFA depending on its UTF-8 encoding.
